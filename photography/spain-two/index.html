Barcelona, Spain

My second day in Spain I mostly spent climbing Monserrat. I took the Aeri de Monserrat cable car early in the morning to the Montserrat Monastery. I spent about an hour to two hours climbing to Sant Jeroni with a girl I met from Quebec, Canada. I went back into Barcelona in the evening to eat and relax and on the way back to my hotel I ran into two little girls playing a game of pretending to sell toys on the street.
