San Miguel de Allende, Mexico

San Miguel de Allende is known as a town where Americans come to retire. It was definetely the "fancy" part of our trip. We did a lot more tourist attractions in this town, visited shops, went to bars, and saw the really beautiful Parroquia de San Miguel Arcángel, a giant church in the center of San Miguel.
