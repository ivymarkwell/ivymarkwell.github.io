Cuerámaro, Mexico

July 28, 2019

My first couple of days in Mexico were spent with my friends' families in León and Cuerámaro. Our second day was in Cuerámaro, where my friends were born and raised. It's a small, very cute town south of León. When we weren't spending time visiting their family we spent time exploring the town, the many shops, and eating amazing food.
