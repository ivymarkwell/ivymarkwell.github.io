Spain Day 1

September 1, 2018

My first day in Barcelona, Spain! I started the morning by going to the La Boqueria, a local market in the center of the city. I walked around and went to the Catalonoia Square where you could feed pigeons and then headed to a reservation I had at Niño Viejo. Afterwards I went to Plaça d'Espanya and Museu Nacional d'Art de Catalunya. On my way back to my hotel to get ready for a local meet up I was attending, I met José and his chickens.
