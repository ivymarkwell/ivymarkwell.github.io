United Kingdom Day 5

September 9, 2018

My last day of my trip was one of the most eventful. I took a free bus to the Brecon National Park an hour north of Cardiff. I ended up getting off at the wrong place with a group of five other people in the middle of the park with no cell service. I was able to eventually catch another bus on the side of the road to the small city I was going to and it was beautiful. I saw the sunset behind the mountains of the park on the small city in Brecon and then I took the bus back to Cardiff.
